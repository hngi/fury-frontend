    ABOUT US

    Luxury hotel is a 5-minute drive from the A4 highway, 5.5 kilometers from Jon Peru International airport and Dickson Brown railyway station.

    We offer 25 modern Classic rooms (Single or double) and 3 suites, including luxurious Presidential Suite. Rooms in Luxury hotel are spacious and air-conditioned. Each of them provides wireless internet, mini bar, coffee making facility, satellite LCD television, bathrobes and a hairdyers. During their stay here, hotel guests can enjoy Wellness Zone of the SPA Center comprising a swimming pool, gym and a large jacuzzi for free.
